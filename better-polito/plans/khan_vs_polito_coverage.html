Mechanics (Khan covers this well)

1D & 2D Kinematics
Position, velocity, acceleration, projectile motion — fully covered
Newton's Laws & Forces
Free-body diagrams, friction, normal force, inclines — fully covered
Work, Energy & Power
Kinetic/potential energy, conservation of energy, energy bar charts — fully covered
Linear Momentum & Collisions
Impulse, elastic/inelastic collisions, center of mass — fully covered
Rotational Dynamics & Rigid Body
Torque, moment of inertia, angular momentum, rolling — fully covered
Simple Harmonic Motion
Spring and pendulum oscillators, period/frequency — fully covered
Fluids
Pressure, buoyancy, continuity, Bernoulli — fully covered
Circular Motion & Gravity
Khan covers circular motion well; but Kepler's laws, gravitational field/potential, and Gauss' theorem for gravity are thin or missing
Non-inertial reference frames
Fictitious forces (Coriolis, centrifugal) — barely touched on Khan

Thermodynamics (Khan Academy AP Physics 1 = NO coverage)

Calorimetry & Heat Transfer
Temperature, specific heat, latent heat — not in AP Physics 1
First Law of Thermodynamics
Internal energy, work done by/on gas, PV diagrams — not covered
Ideal Gases & Kinetic Theory
Gas laws, molecular model of pressure — not covered
Second Law & Entropy
Carnot cycle, heat engines, efficiency, entropy — not covered

Other Polito topics not on Khan

Error Theory & Uncertainty Propagation
Measurement errors, statistical vs systematic — use Taylor's "Introduction to Error Analysis"
Polar & cylindrical coordinates, radius of curvature
Khan uses only Cartesian — Polito expects these for circular/curvilinear motion
